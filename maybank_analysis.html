Analysis Summary

Strengths: CET1 14.9% ROE 11.7% Div. Yield 6.0% LCR 141.2% Asset Quality Improving

Maybank delivered a record net profit of RM10.51B in FY2025 (+4.2% YoY), driven by steady net fund-based income growth, disciplined cost management, and improving asset quality. ROE strengthened to 11.7% and the bank maintained its ~6% dividend yield — among the highest in the sector. Capital & liquidity positions remain robust (CET1 14.9%, LCR 141.2%) well above regulatory minimums. The bank exceeded all four of its sustainability targets.

Risks: NIM Pressure Cost Inflation Regional Rate Cuts Indonesia Portfolio
NIM held steady at 2.05% but faces pressure from ongoing regional rate cuts. Overhead costs rose 2.6% due to IT spending, marketing, and personnel inflation. Loan growth in Indonesia remains soft as the portfolio rebalances. Revenue (operating) declined 3.7% due to changes in revenue composition.
