Investment Summary

Lygend Resources (2245.HK) — A leading nickel full-chain company with operations spanning trading, HPAL wet-process smelting, and RKEF pyrometallurgy on Obi Island, Indonesia. FY2025 was a breakout year: revenue RMB 40.24B (+37.7%), attributable net profit RMB 2.857B (+61.1%). The ONC HPAL project reached full capacity, KPS pyrometallurgy progressively commissioning, and the EV battery materials segment driving premium margins.

Strengths: Revenue +37.7% Profit +61.1% ROE 24.6% Cost Leader EV Battery Play A-Share IPO Catalyst

Risks: Debt/Equity 89% Nickel Price Risk Indonesia Policy Environmental Scrutiny Stock -55% from High
