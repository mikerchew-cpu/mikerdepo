Analysis Summary

Strengths: Revenue +23.6% Auto +185% Data Center +33.8% ROE 19.1% GS target RMB 106 OpenAI partnership

Luxshare delivered a stellar FY2025 with record revenue (RMB 332.3B) and profit (RMB 16.6B), both up ~24%. The Leoni acquisition transformed the automotive segment into a powerhouse (+185%), while AI infrastructure drove data center growth (+33.8%). The company deepened its Apple relationship while diversifying into auto, AI infrastructure, and an OpenAI device partnership. At P/S of 0.86x and P/E of 17x (forward 14.5x), the stock screens as undervalued relative to its growth profile. Goldman Sachs has a RMB 106 target price.

Risks: Apple concentration Debt/Equity 101% Negative FCF Low margins (5%) US-China tariffs Share -16% 52wk
Heavy Apple dependency (~80% consumer exposure) creates single-client risk. Debt/Equity at 101.5% post-Leoni is elevated. Free cash flow turned negative due to heavy CapEx for expansion. Gross margins at 11.6% reflect the high-volume, low-margin manufacturing model. US-China trade tensions and supply chain regionalization pose ongoing headwinds. Despite strong earnings, the stock is down 16% over 52 weeks.
