Analysis Summary

Strengths: Revenue +25% Adj. Profit +43.8% EV Profitable R&D 7.2% IoT Record 754M MAU

Xiaomi delivered a landmark FY2025 with record revenue of RMB 457.3B (+25%) and record adjusted net profit of RMB 39.2B (+43.8%). The EV & AI segment crossed RMB 100B in revenue for the first time (+223.8%) and achieved its first annual EBIT profit (+RMB 0.9B), a remarkable milestone just two years after first delivery. Smartphone remained solid at #3 globally (13.3% share), IoT hit records (+18.3%), and internet services maintained high margins at 76.5%. R&D spending accelerated to RMB 33.1B (+37.8%), targeting >RMB 200B over 5 years. The "Human × Car × Home" ecosystem is now fully operational.

Risks: Q1 Profit Warning Stock -55% from High Memory Cost Inflation EV Competition No Dividend
Q1 2026 net profit expected -43% to -57% YoY due to memory costs and EV ramp investment. Stock trades at HK$26.88, down 55% from 52-week high of HK$59.90. Intense competition in both smartphone (Apple, Samsung) and EV (BYD, Tesla, Nio) markets. No dividend — all capital reinvested into growth.
