Investment Summary

Xiaomi (1810.HK) — Successfully transitioning from smartphone maker to "Human × Car × Home" tech ecosystem. FY2025 was a landmark year: record revenue (RMB 457.3B, +25%), record adjusted profit (RMB 39.2B, +43.8%), and the EV & AI segment crossing RMB 100B while turning profitable for the first time (EBIT +RMB 0.9B). Smartphone business remains solid (#3 globally, 13.3% share), IoT is hitting records (+18.3%), and the EV business delivered 411K vehicles (+200%) with a 550K target for 2026.

Key Catalysts: EV profitability inflection, SU7 Ultra premium validation (~RMB 500K+), YU7 SUV dominance, 550K delivery target, global IoT expansion, R&D (RMB 105.5B cumulative, targeting RMB 200B in 5 years).

Risks: Q1 2026 profit decline (net income -43-57% YoY due to memory costs + EV ramp), smartphone ASP pressure, memory chip cost inflation, EV investment burn rate, stock -55% from 52-week high (HK$59.90 to HK$26.88), no dividend, intense competition in both smartphone (Apple, Samsung) and EV (BYD, Tesla, Nio) markets.
